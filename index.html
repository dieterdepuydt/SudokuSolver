Sudoku Solver

This little program, written in jQuery/Javascript (with a flavor of HTML5) solves SUDOKU's, using the (inefficient) backtrack algorithm.

Help?!
  • Click a square to select it and enter a number.
  • The next square will be activated automatically.
  • Enter a new number or enter a space to leave the square empty and activate the next square.
  • To change the content of a square, activate the square and enter the new number (or use space to make the square empty).
  • Click "Solve!" to solve the puzzle.
